# Privacy Policy — Martial Body

_Last updated: 2026-04-19_

Martial Body ("the app") is a single-developer Android application that
delivers a 24-week MMA training program and tracks your workouts locally on
your device.

## What data the app collects

Martial Body does **not** collect, transmit, or share any personal data with
the developer or any third party. The app has no accounts, no sign-in, no
analytics, no crash reporting, and no advertising SDKs.

Data you enter — including your name, age, sex, body measurements, and
workout log entries — is stored **only** in a local SQLite database inside
the app's private sandbox on your device. It is not uploaded anywhere.

## Permissions

The app does not request any Android runtime permissions. The only
network-adjacent action available is an optional tap to open an external
YouTube search URL for exercise demonstrations, which is handled by your
device's browser — the app itself does not make network requests.

## Backups

Android cloud backup and device-to-device transfer are disabled for this
app. If you reinstall the app or switch devices, your training data will not
carry over automatically. Use **Profile → Export workout log (CSV)** to save
a copy before reinstalling.

## Children

The app is intended for adults preparing for combat sports. It is not
directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information
from them.

## Data retention and deletion

All data lives on your device. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes your
training data. There is no server-side copy.
